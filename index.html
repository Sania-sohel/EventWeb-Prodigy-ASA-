About the event:

National institute of technology, Warangal, will be hosting a three-day cultural fest. This is a new event, held starting this year, held by students of NITW, inviting students of other colleges to participate as well. Students can get to know about people from different cultural backgrounds and interests.

These healthy interactions not only enhance the students' interpersonal and communication skills but also prepare them for the real world. These events also help students to cultivate skills like planning, teamwork, leadership, and multi-tasking. It also helps students to learn how to balance personal and professional life.

All these highlighting aspects of college fests in a student’s life go on to show why the significance of college fests in a student’s life is so clear-cut in modern times.
